CODEX

I. The Umbral Depths were not made. They were found, beneath the ruined chapel at Falkhart, in the year 1622.


II. What lies below does not obey the laws of stone or time. Each descent carves its own shape. Each vessel who enters returns changed, or does not return.


III. The Order of Ash tends the threshold. They are forbidden to enter. They record what the returned whisper, and burn the rest.


IV. It is said that at the lowest floor — the twelfth, though some count more — sits an Eye, unblinking. To meet its gaze is to be remembered. To be remembered is to become part of the Depths.


V. The souls of the fallen settle in the Sanctum's basin. The living may drink of them, and carry that strength down with them. This is called harvest, though no one is certain who harvests whom.


VI. Bring light. Bring blades. Bring more lantern oil than you think you will need.


VII. Do not speak to the Whisperers. Do not look into the Still Pools. Do not trust doors that are already open.


VIII. If you hear your own voice in the dark, do not answer.
