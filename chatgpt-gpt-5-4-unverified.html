How to Survive

The run ends on death. Use the dark wisely.

Light matters. Darkness raises terror. Use your lantern sparingly and focus it with right click to cut through thick fog.
Every floor changes. Objectives, enemies, layouts, and rewards shift every run.
Leveling is immediate. Killing horrors and recovering essence grants XP. On level-up you choose one of three powers.
Shrines are safe, but not free. Restore yourself, then spend essence to claim relic blessings.
Ammo is pressure. Your knife never runs dry. The revolver wins breathing room. Reload before the next sound in the dark.
Finish the floor objective. Exit doors unlock only after sigils are found, altars are destroyed, or the hunt target is slain.
